About Us

At Campfire Labs, we are a passionate team of roughly 10 talented individuals committed to crafting engaging and innovative gaming experiences. Our mission is to captivate players around the world by bringing new technologies and immersive worlds to life.

With over 2.6 million plays globally, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in gaming. We believe in the power of collaboration and the magic that happens when creative minds come together.

At Campfire Labs, we pride ourselves on being a community-first studio. We actively engage with our players, valuing their feedback as a vital part of our creative process. By listening to their experiences and insights, we continuously refine and improve our games, ensuring they remain fun, immersive, and player-focused. For us, building a strong connection with our community is as important as the worlds we create.
